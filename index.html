Kartoteket is a small company making systems for presentation and dissemination of data and content. Our core tool is Pipalbot.

We make

Pipalbot is a modern day index card system. With extras. It is an online, content agnostic database that stores just about any tabular data. The data is organised by its time and space dimensions, revealing temporal and spatial relationships.

Relationships enables analysis of patterns and interactions, transforming data to knowledge. By connecting the dots, Pipalbot paints you the picture.
